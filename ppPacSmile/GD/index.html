❓ PacSmile Guide
🎯 Objective: Collect fruits 🍎 in the maze and deliver them to your counterpart.
🕹️ Controls: ⌨️ Arrows / WASD — 📱 D-Pad touch — 👆 Swipe — ⏸ Space/Esc to pause
⚡ POWER-UPS
🔵 Pill — Enemies frightened for a few seconds, you can eat them!
⭐ Turbo — Speed doubled temporarily
🍬 Candy — Score x2 for a few seconds
🛡️ Shield — Absorbs one enemy hit
💊 Medikit — Restores 38 HP
❤️ Extra life — Gain an additional life
🗝️ Key — Unlocks a locked passage 🔒
💡 Bulb — Reveals secret passages
💣 Bomb — Eliminates all visible enemies
🧊 Ice — Freezes all enemies for a few seconds
😠 ENEMIES
Level 1 has 2 angry enemies. Each level adds one, up to 10.
💨 FROM LEVEL 7
The counterpart runs away!
🛒 SHOP & COINS
Earn 🪙 coins by collecting fruits, eating enemies, and completing levels. Open the Shop from the toolbar (🛒) to buy power-ups — prices rise each purchase. Each item has a 📺 Free button to get one via Ad watch.
⚡ POWER-UP BAR
The 8 slots at the bottom of the game screen show your inventory. Power-ups collected in the maze stack with purchased ones. Tap a slot (or press keys 1–8 on keyboard) to activate. An active effect shows a countdown timer.
💡 TIPS
• Enemies hide at the start — use the time to grab power-ups!
• Use 💣 Bomb when surrounded
• Use ⭐ Turbo to escape or chase the counterpart
• Combo: eat enemies back-to-back for multiplied points
• 📺 Watch Ads to get free power-ups, extra coins, or revive on Game Over!
