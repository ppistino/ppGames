🏗️ How to Play
👁️ Observation (10s): Study the intact city carefully — roads, buildings, houses. Memorize everything before the asteroid hits!
☄️ Asteroid Strike: The roads are destroyed. Your mission: put them back using the puzzle pieces in the toolbar.
🧩 Pieces: The toolbar holds all the pieces needed to reconstruct the roads exactly. Select a piece, drag it onto the grid. Tap a piece in the toolbar to rotate it 90°.
↩️ Pick up: Tap a piece already placed on the grid to return it to the toolbar.
🚗 Vehicles: As soon as a continuous road connects a building (🏥🏪…) to a house (🏠🏡…), a vehicle departs and earns you 💰 on arrival.
⭐ Stars: 1⭐ First vehicle arrives · 2⭐ All vehicles arrive · 3⭐ Every vehicle reaches its correct destination (each house has a specific vehicle assigned — check the badge above it!)
♻️ Recycle: Pay 10💰 to swap a piece you can't place. Use sparingly!
🗺️ Map Hint: Purchase in the Shop to see where each piece belongs on the city map.
🐛 Debug: CTRL+D → infinite coins.
