🚀 ppDeepOrbit
🎯 Objective: Launch from Earth, blast through the asteroid belt, collect resources on alien planets, and land safely back home. Each mission earns credits — spend them on powerful upgrades for the next run.
🗺️ MISSION PHASES
1. 🌍 Launch — Thrust off the launch pad. Angle up to escape Earth's gravity.
2. ☄️ Asteroid Belt — Shoot through the belt. Collect resource drops ⚡💜🔩⚙️.
3. 🌑 Planet Surface — Land on the refuel pad to restore fuel & HP. Fly around collecting terrain 🌿🪸🟡🍄 and ocean 🐚💧🦑 resources. Return once your quota is filled.
4. ☄️ Return Journey — Fight back through the belt with heavier enemy waves.
5. 🎯 Landing — Descend carefully. Stay vertical (±10°) and slow. Three outcomes: 🟢 Perfect · 🟡 Hard landing (−HP) · 🔴 Crash (−HP, retry).
6. 🛒 Mission Complete — Cargo is sold automatically. Buy upgrades, then launch the next mission.
🕹️ CONTROLS
⌨️ W / ↑ Thrust  ·  A / ← Rotate left  ·  D / → Rotate right
⌨️ S / ↓ Brake (travel)  ·  Space / F Fire weapons
⌨️ 1–8 Activate equipped upgrades  ·  P Pause & save
📱 D-Pad to navigate · 🔥 Fire button · 🛒 Quick shop · Upgrade buttons
⛽ FUEL & ❤️ HULL
Fuel depletes when thrusting. Collect ⚡ Energy Crystals in space, or dock on a planet refuel pad to restore fuel and hull integrity slowly.
Hull drops when hit by asteroids or aliens. If it hits 0% — GAME OVER.
💎 RESOURCES & CREDITS
Space drops: ⚡ Crystal (+fuel) · 🔩 Iron · ⚙️ Gear · 💜 Xenite (+HP) · ☢️ Nuclearite · 💎 Diamond · ❤️ Life Cell (+HP)
Planet terrain: 🌿 Alien Flora · 🪸 Rock Crystal · 🟡 Gold · 🍄 Fungus
Planet ocean: 🐚 Plasma Shell · 💧 Pure Water · 🦑 Essence
All cargo is auto-sold at mission end. Credits fund upgrades.
👾 ENEMIES
☄️ Asteroids — split when shot; small fragments score more points
🛸 Alien Fighters — appear at level 3+, shoot back at you
🛰️ Satellites — appear at level 5+. DO NOT SHOOT — penalty −200 pts!
⚙️ UPGRADES (8 available)
🛡️ Shield · 🔫 Double Cannon · ⚡ Ionic Engine · 🧲 Tractor Beam
🔋 Fuel Boost · 📡 Deep Radar · 🔧 Repair Kit · 💉 Medical Kit
Buy in the post-mission shop or mid-mission via the 🛒 button. Activate with keys 1–8 or the on-screen upgrade bar.
💾 SAVING
Press P or the ⏸ button to pause — the game auto-saves. Next time you open the game, a ▶ Continue button will appear on the start screen.
