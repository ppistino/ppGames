🚀 ppDeepOrbit
🎯 Objective: Launch from Earth, survive the asteroid belt, collect resources on alien planets, and return safely to sell your cargo and upgrade your ship.
🗺️ MISSION PHASES
1. Launch — Lift off from Earth. Watch the planet shrink behind you.
2. Asteroid Belt — Dodge and destroy asteroids. Collect resource drops ⚡💜🔩.
3. Planet Orbit — Enter the target planet's atmosphere.
4. Planet Surface — Collect terrain 🌿🪸🟡 and ocean 🐚💧🦑 resources.
5. Return Journey — Fight back through the asteroid belt with more enemies.
6. Landing — Control descent speed. Land gently on the platform!
7. Debrief — Sell cargo, buy upgrades, advance to the next level.
🕹️ CONTROLS
⌨️ W/↑ Thrust forward   A/← Rotate left
⌨️ D/→ Rotate right   S/↓ Brake
⌨️ Space / F Fire
📱 D-Pad Navigate   🔥 Fire button Shoot
⛽ FUEL
Fuel is finite! Running out = GAME OVER. Collect ⚡ Crystals from asteroids to refuel. Upgrade your engine to reduce consumption.
💎 RESOURCES
Space: ⚡ Energy Crystal · 🔩 Iron · 💜 Xenite · ☢️ Nuclearite
Terrain: 🌿 Flora · 🪸 Crystal · 🟡 Gold · 🍄 Fungus
Ocean: 🐚 Shell · 💧 Pure Water · 🦑 Essence
👾 ENEMIES (scale with level)
☄️ Asteroids — split into smaller pieces when shot
🛸 Alien fighters — appear at level 3+ and shoot back
🛰️ Satellites — DO NOT SHOOT or lose points!
⚙️ UPGRADES
Buy upgrades in the Debrief screen: shields, double cannon, fuel tank, magnet beam and more.
