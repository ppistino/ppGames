❓ How to Play
🎯 Goal: Clear the board by matching identical emoji tiles. A tile is free (glowing) when it is not covered from above and has at least one open side (left or right).
🀄 Classic — match 2 identical tiles. The standard mode, great for beginners.
🔱 Trio — match 3 identical tiles at once. Harder to find groups, more rewarding.
💎 Quad — match 4 identical tiles. Maximum challenge, big combos.
⚡ Arcade — new tiles fall onto the board every 12 seconds. Clear fast before the board overflows!
🌸 Zen — no timer, no pressure. Play at your own pace.
💣 Blitz — 60 second countdown. Match as many as you can before time runs out!
🪙 Coin tiles: Match a full group of 🪙 → +10🪙 bonus per tile! — They behave like normal tiles.
💎 Diamond tiles: Match a full group of 💎 → +100🪙 bonus per tile! — Rare and very valuable.
🏆 Score: Each match = 100 pts × current level. Chain matches quickly to build a combo multiplier up to ×10 for massive bonus points. Clearing the board awards +2000 pts × level.
↩ Undo — puts the last matched group back on the board. Up to 3 undos free per game.
💡 Hint — highlights a valid matching group. 3 free per game.
🔀 Shuffle — reshuffles all remaining tiles into a brand new random layout, same emojis. 2 free per game.
💣 Bomb — destroys a complete matching group, even buried ones (X-Ray!). 1 free per game.
🤖 AI Solver — lets the computer play for you. Buy in the 🛒 Shop. Tap again to stop it. Note: the AI may make mistakes and might not fully solve the board — it also earns only 50% of normal score and coins!
⭐ Levels: Each level adds more tiles (starting ~36, capping at 144) and taller stacks (+1 layer every 3 levels, no ceiling!). At high levels expect towers 5-10 tiles tall. Your score always carries over.
