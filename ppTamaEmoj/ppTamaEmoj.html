❓ TamaEmoji — Guide
🥚 What is TamaEmoji?
A digital companion that lives with you in real time. Born from an egg, it grows, develops a unique personality, works, studies, travels, falls ill, ages and eventually passes away — leaving an heir. No two companions are the same.
🌱 Life Phases
🥚 Egg — Hatches after a few seconds. Tap to speed up.
🐣 Infant (days 0–5) — Very fragile. Prioritise feeding, sleep and hugs.
🧒 Teen (days 6–15) — Curious and energetic. Best phase to study and build intelligence.
👤 Adult (days 16–40) — Productive. Work, travel and socialise to earn money and purpose.
👴 Elder (day 40+) — Slower but wise. Focus on meditation, rest and legacy.
📊 Stats — what they mean
🍔 Hunger — Rises over time. Feed regularly or health drops fast.
⚡ Energy — Consumed by every action. Sleep to recover.
😊 Happiness — Drops when lonely or bored. Play, hug and talk.
🚿 Hygiene — Slowly decreases. Low hygiene causes stress and illness.
❤️ Health — Falls if hunger, hygiene or stress are critical. Use medicine when sick.
🧠 Intelligence — Grows with study. Unlocks better jobs and dialogue.
💰 Money — Earned by working. Needed for travel and special events.
😰 Stress — Rises with work and neglect. Rest and play to reduce.
🌟 Self-esteem — Boosted by compliments and achievements. Affects happiness regeneration.
🎯 Purpose — Long-term wellbeing. Built by meditation, travel and balance.
🎮 Actions — categories
Care — Feed 🍔, Sleep 😴, Clean 🚿, Medicine 💊 — cover basic needs.
Activity — Sport 🏃, Play 🎮, Music 🎵, Watch 📺 — fun and energy.
Mind — Meditate 🧘, Study 📚, Hobby 🎨 — intelligence and purpose.
Social — Hug 🤗, Talk 💬, Compliment 💝 — happiness and self-esteem.
Career — Work 💼, Side job 🔧 — earn money, build purpose.
Life — Travel ✈️, Event 🎪, Birthday 🎂 — special milestones.
🧬 Personality Traits
Each companion has 5 traits generated at birth. They influence how stats change with each action and what your Tama says.
😄 Humour · 🔥 Ambition · 💗 Sensitivity · 🔍 Curiosity · 🤝 Social Energy
Traits are inherited by heirs — with slight mutations.
⚠️ Crisis & Death
If Health reaches 0 or Happiness stays critically low for too long, your companion passes away. An heir is automatically generated, inheriting personality, a name, and some of your intelligence bonus.
Urgent alerts appear in the 🔔 notification panel — don't ignore them!
☀️ Day / Night Cycle
The sky inside the creature panel follows real clock time. Sun rises at 6:00, sets at 18:00. At night: moon and stars appear. Your Tama gets tired naturally after 21:00 — sleeping then costs less energy.
🌍 Language & 🔊 Voice
Switch language anytime from the toolbar (🌍) during the game — IT · EN · FR · DE · ES.

Voice engine: Web Speech API — integrata nel browser, nessun server, funziona offline.
La qualità e le lingue disponibili dipendono dal sistema operativo:
 • iOS / macOS → voci Apple (motore Siri) — qualità alta, IT/EN/FR/DE/ES ben supportate
 • Windows / Edge → voci Microsoft Neural — buona qualità
 • Android → voci Google TTS — dipende da cosa è installato nel dispositivo

Genere voce (👩/👨): il pulsante appare nella toolbar solo se il tuo sistema ha sia una voce femminile che maschile per la lingua selezionata. Se non vedi il pulsante significa che per quella lingua è disponibile una sola voce.

Nota: il primo messaggio viene prima tradotto nella lingua selezionata, poi letto — così la voce è sempre allineata al testo visualizzato.
⊞ Float Widget & ⊡ Compact Mode
⊞ Float Widget — A small draggable panel, always visible. Shows stats, quick actions and your Tama's mood. Minimises to a tiny bar.
⊡ Compact Mode — Opens a slim sidebar window (or panel) synced in real time with the main game. Useful for multitasking.
💡 Tips & Strategy
• Balance work and rest — burnout permanently drains purpose
• Study intensively during Teen phase — intelligence carries over to heirs
• Talk/hug daily — happiness decay accelerates if ignored for long
• Travel once in a while — gives a big purpose and happiness boost
• Real time matters: leaving your Tama alone overnight will hurt their stats
• The longer a companion lives, the richer the heir bonus
