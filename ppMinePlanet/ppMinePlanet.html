❓ HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Explore the hex planet, collect all required resources shown in the right panel, then reach the 🌀 PORTAL and press COLLECT to complete the level.

🖱 CONTROLS

Tap/click a cell to select it. Use the buttons at the bottom to act:

• 🔍 REVEAL — uncovers the selected cell. If it's a mine, you lose HP!

• ⛏ COLLECT — gathers a resource from a revealed cell into your inventory.

• 🚩 FLAG — marks a suspected mine. Flagged cells cannot be revealed.

⚠ MINES

Revealing a 💣 MINE costs HP. Each cell shows a number: adjacent mine count. Use it to deduce safe cells. Water hides 💣 WATER MINES too!

✨ SPECIAL ITEMS

• 💥 BOMB — reveals all neighbors (may hit mines for half damage)

• 🗺️ MAP — reveals several random safe terrain cells

• 🧪 POTION — 60% chance to heal, 40% chance to damage

• 🔦 LANTERN — safely illuminates all adjacent cells (no mine damage)

• 🩺 MEDKIT — restores HP when revealed

📊 PANELS

Left panel shows MINES present on the map, flags placed, and mines accidentally hit. Right panel shows the OBJECTIVE: collected / needed (available on map).

🏆 SCORING

+5 per revealed cell, +20×level per collected resource, +200×level for completing a level. Each level increases difficulty!
